### Unary Operators

addition     : +4
subtraction  : -4
increment    : x++
decrement    : y--
not          : !x

### Binary Operators
addition         :  3 + 5
subtraction  	 :  4 - 2
multiplication   :  2 * 6
division         :  8 / 2
less than		 :  7 < 13
less or equal    :  x <= 13
greater than     :  9 > 2
greater or equal :  y >= 0
equal			 :  x == y
not equal		 :  x != y
logical and      :  x and y
logical or       :  x or y



### Ternary Operators

condition? exp1: exp2   :   5 > 4? true: false
